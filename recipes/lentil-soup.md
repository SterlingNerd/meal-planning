# Hearty Lentil Soup

|               |   |
|---------------|---|
| Rating        | 4 |
| Ease          | 5 |
| Quickness     | 2 |
| Affordability | 5 |

A comforting, protein-rich soup that's very budget-friendly. Great for meal prep and leftovers.

# Ingredients

|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
|Brown Lentils|1.5 cups|Rinsed|
|Onion|1 medium|Diced|
|Carrots|2-3|Diced|
|Celery|2-3 stalks|Diced|
|Garlic|3-4 cloves|Minced|
|Vegetable Broth|6-8 cups|Or water with bouillon|
|Canned Diced Tomatoes|1 can|14.5 oz|
|Italian Seasoning||
|Bay Leaves|2|Optional|
|Oil||
|Salt and Pepper||

# Directions

- Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat
- Add onion, carrots, and celery, cook until softened
- Add garlic and Italian seasoning, cook for 1 minute
- Add lentils, broth, tomatoes, and bay leaves
- Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer
- Cook until lentils are tender, about 30-40 minutes
- Season with salt and pepper to taste
- Remove bay leaves before serving

# Other Considerations

Can add spinach or kale at the end. Very forgiving recipe - can add more broth if too thick, or cook longer if too thin. Great with crusty bread. Can make a large batch and freeze portions.
