# Ingredients
|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
| Imagine Portobello Mushroom Creamy Soup | 1 Box | 
| Sliced Mushrooms | 1 Pint | 
| Pasta | |
| Corn Starch | 2 tbsp | To thicken soup
| Chicken Thighs 

# Directions
- Cook Pasta according to directions on box
- Pour soup in a pot, simmer on low
- In a pan, cook mushrooms, add to soup
- In the same pan, cook chicken until almost done
- Remove chicken, chop and add to soup.
- combine cornstarch with water to make a slurry, stir into soup to thicken
- drain pasta, retaining a small amount of pasta water 
- Combine all ingredients and serve
