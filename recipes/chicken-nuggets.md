|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|

# Directions

## Cooking
- Follow package directions for cooking ya silly!
- Probably add 10%-20% cooking time because most ovens lie.

## Serve
- Let cool slightly and serve with your favorite dipping sauce
