|               |   |
|---------------|---|
| Rating        | 4 |
| Ease          | 4 |
| Quickness     | 4 |
| Affordability | 4 |

# Ingredients
|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
| Frozen Veggies | 1 bag
| Meat | | Typically pre-cooked frozen
| Sauce | | Typically soy sauce, or teriyaki base.
| Oil

# Directions
- Heat wok
- Sear meat
- Add veggies, cover to steam
- Once veggies are half way done, remove lid add sauce
- stir till done
