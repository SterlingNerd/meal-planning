# Ingredients
|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
| Imagine Portobello Mushroom Creamy Soup | 1 Box | 
| Sliced Mushrooms | 1 Pint | 
| Corn Starch | 2 tbsp | To thicken soup
| Pork Chops
| Potatoes
| Milk alternative
| Garlic

# Directions

## Potatoes
- fill a pot with **cold** water.
- wash and cut potatoes into equal sized chunks and put in pot
- add salt and bring potatos to a boil
- when fork-tender, drain mash with butter, garlic, and milk

## Meat
- Pour soup in a pot, simmer on low
- In a pan, cook mushrooms, add to soup
- In the same pan, sear meat
- combine cornstarch with water to make a slurry, stir into soup to thicken
- Combine all ingredients in oven-safe dish
- bake until meat is cooked
