# Creamy Corn, Quinoa, and Potato Soup

||||
|-|-------|
| Rating | 5 |
| Ease | 3 |
| Quickness | 3 |
| Affordability | 4 |

A hearty vegetarian soup featuring corn, quinoa, and potatoes in a creamy base of rice and cashew milk, seasoned with various herbs.

# Ingredients

|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
|Onions|3|Chopped|
|Celery|1/2 bunch|Chopped|
|Rice Milk|2 boxes|Approx 8 cups total|
|Cashew Milk|1 box|Approx 4 cups total|
|Corn|6 cups||
|Quinoa|1 1/2 cups||
|Potatoes|5 cups|Cut|
|Onion & Herb Seasoning|1 tablespoon||
|Garlic & Herb Seasoning|1 tablespoon||
|Real Salt|1 teaspoon||
|Lite Salt|2 tablespoons||
|Basil|1 teaspoon||
|Oregano|1 teaspoon||
|Thyme|1 teaspoon||
|Parsley|1 teaspoon||

# Directions

## Cooking
1. Saute the chopped onions and celery.
2. Add rice milk, cashew milk, corn, quinoa, and cut potatoes to the pot.
3. Stir in all seasonings: onion & herb, garlic & herb, real salt, lite salt, basil, oregano, thyme, and parsley.
4. Let the soup simmer to finish cooking.

## Serve
- Serve hot
