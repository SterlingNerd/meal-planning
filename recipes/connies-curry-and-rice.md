# Connie's Curry & Rice

|||
|-|-------|
| Rating | 4 |
| Ease | 3 |

A hearty vegetarian curry with vegetables, garbanzo beans, and optional eggs, served over rice or quinoa. The curry is made with rice milk and brown rice flour for a creamy, dairy-free sauce.

# Ingredients

|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
|Olive Oil|2 tablespoons||
|Onions|3 cups|Chopped|
|Cauliflower|4 cups|Chopped|
|Broccoli|4 cups|Chopped|
|Mixed Mushrooms|2 cups|Chopped (alternative or addition to broccoli)|
|Curry Powder|1 tablespoon|Add before milk|
|Brown Rice Flour|1/4 cup||
|Rice Milk|1 box (4 cups)||
|Morton Lite Salt|1/2 tablespoon||
|Mrs. Dash, Onion + Herb|1 teaspoon||
|Curry Powder|1/2 tablespoon|Add after milk|
|Frozen Petite Peas|1 lb||
|Garbanzo Beans|1 can (15.5 oz)|S.W. brand, low sodium|
|Eggs|3|Optional - can use 3 egg whites or 3-6 whole eggs|
|Peanut Butter|To taste|Optional|
|Rice or Quinoa|For serving||

# Directions

## Cooking
1. Saute onions, mushrooms, cauliflower, and broccoli in olive oil. Add 1 tablespoon curry powder to the saute.
2. Add rice milk, and slowly stir in brown rice flour as the milk heats.
3. Add seasonings (Morton Lite Salt and Mrs. Dash) and simmer.
4. Add frozen peas and bring back to heat.
5. If using eggs, scramble them (and/or egg whites). Add garbanzo beans and scrambled eggs to the curry.
6. Add peanut butter to taste if desired.

## Serve
- Serve over rice or quinoa
