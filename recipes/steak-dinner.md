The classic. Steak, mushrooms, baked potato.

# Ingredients
|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
|Steak | 2 |
|Chicken Breast | 1 | Could be thighs or something else.
|Potatoes| | 
|Mushrooms | 1 Pint |
|Oyester Sauce|1 tbsp
| butter |
| bacon bits | | Optional for potato
| chives | | Optional for potato

# Directions
- 
- Wrap potatoes in foil, bake
- Cook Steak
- In a separate pan Cook mushrooms
    - When almost done, add oyster sauce


# Other Considerations
Typically when we make this meal, we make a chicken breast for mom as she often avoids red meat.
