# Pasta Salad

|||
|-|-------|
| Rating | 5 |
| Ease | 4 |
| Quickness | 3 |
| Affordability | 4 |

A delicios meal for anyone that is pretty to make. It has pasta, vegitables, and a yummy Italian dressing and it gets marinated for a while.

|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
| Pasta | 1 Box | Typically Rotini |
| Broccoli | 2 Heads |
| Carrots | 3-4 |
| Cauliflower | 1 Head |
| Italian Dressing | 1 Bottle|

# Direction
- Cook the pasta in large pot
- Chop veggies
- Drain pasta and rinse with cold water to stop cooking
- Place all in a large bowl and mix in dressing
- Put in fridge for a few hours to marinate
