# Artichoke Soup

||||
|-|-------|
| Rating | 4 |
| Ease | 3 |
| Quickness | 3 |
| Affordability | 3 |

A hearty vegetable soup with a variety of mushrooms, artichokes, and dried tomatoes. Serves a crowd (16-20 servings). Can be served with sour cream or cream for added richness.

# Ingredients

|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
|Olive Oil|4 tablespoons||
|Shallots|2 cups|Sliced|
|White Mushrooms|1 package (10 oz)|Cut up|
|Crimini Mushrooms|1 package (10 oz)|Cut up|
|Shiitake Mushrooms|2 packages (4 oz each)|Cut up|
|Artichoke Hearts|30 pieces (approx 2 jars)|Cut up|
|Carrots|2 cups|Shredded|
|Brown Rice Flour|4 tablespoons||
|Dried Thyme|1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon|Dried, crushed|
|Crushed Red Pepper|1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon||
|Garlic Powder|1/2 - 3/4 teaspoon|
|Low Sodium Vegetable Broth|2 boxes (8 cups)||
|Low Sodium V8|3/4 - 1 cup|
|Oil Packed Dried Tomatoes|1/4 cup|Chopped|
|Bay Leaf|1||
|Green Onions|For garnish|Cut in strips|
|Sour Cream or Cream|For serving|Optional|

# Directions

## Cooking
1. Saute in oil: shallots, mushrooms, and carrots until tender.
2. Stir in seasonings and flour.
3. Add broth.
4. Stir in tomatoes, artichokes, and bay leaf.
5. Simmer for 15 minutes, covered.
6. Discard bay leaf.

## Serve
- Top with green onions
- Can serve with sour cream or cream if desired
