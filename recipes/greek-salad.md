| Ease | 4 |

A greek inspired salad. Great for a side, or pig out and eat the whole bowl as a main.

# Ingredients
|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
| Cucumber | 1-2 |
| Onion | 1/2 medium |
| Cherry/Grape Tomatoes | 1 pint
| Feta Cheese |
| Greek Olives |
| Pepperoncini |
| Olive Oil | 2 tbsp
| Basil |

# Directions
- Start by pouring oil in a large mixing bowl
- Add basic and other herbs if desired
- Chop veggies and add to bowl
- Add cheese
- Mix
- Cover and chill for an hour
