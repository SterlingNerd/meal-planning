# Black Bean Bowl

|               |   |
|---------------|---|
| Rating        | 4 |
| Ease          | 5 |
| Quickness     | 4 |
| Affordability | 5 |

A healthy, protein-rich meal that's very budget-friendly. Great for using up leftover vegetables.

# Ingredients

|Ingredient|Amount|Notes|
|-|-|-|
|Black Beans|2 cans|Or 1.5 cups cooked|
|Rice|1.5 cups|Cooked|
|Corn|1 cup|Frozen or canned|
|Bell Peppers|1-2|Diced|
|Onion|1|Diced|
|Tomatoes|2|Diced|
|Cilantro|1 bunch|Chopped|
|Lime|1-2|Juiced|
|Cumin||
|Chili Powder||
|Garlic Powder||
|Oil||
|Salt and Pepper||

# Directions

- Cook rice according to package directions
- Heat oil in a pan over medium heat
- Add onion and bell peppers, cook until softened
- Add black beans (drained if canned), corn, and spices
- Heat through, season with salt and pepper
- To serve, layer rice, bean mixture, tomatoes, and cilantro
- Drizzle with lime juice

# Other Considerations

Can add pre-cooked chicken or other protein. Very customizable - add whatever vegetables are available. Can make a large batch and use for meal prep. For extra flavor, can add salsa or hot sauce.
