# Shopping List

## Produce
- Onions (3 cups chopped + 1/2 medium + 1 whole = ~4-5 medium onions)
- Cauliflower (4 cups, chopped)
- Broccoli (4 cups chopped + 1 head + 1 head = ~2-3 heads total)
- Mixed Mushrooms (2 cups, chopped) - optional
- Carrots (3-4 + 2-3 = ~5-7 carrots)
- Brussels Sprouts (1 lb)
- Potatoes (2-3)
- Cucumber (1-2)
- Cherry/Grape Tomatoes (1 pint)
- Bell Peppers (2)
- Garlic (2-3 cloves)
- Ginger (1 inch)
- Basil

## Meat & Protein
- Chicken Breast (2-3)
- Chicken Thighs (2-3)
- Protein for stir-fry (1-1.5 lbs) - Chicken, pork, or pre-cooked

## Dairy & Eggs
- Eggs (3, optional)
- Feta Cheese

- Olive Oil (2 tablespoons + 2 tbsp + general use)
- Oil (for cooking)
- Rice Milk (1 box, 4 cups)
- Brown Rice Flour (1/4 cup)
- Curry Powder (1 tablespoon + 1/2 tablespoon = 1.5 tablespoons total)
- Morton Lite Salt (1/2 tablespoon)
- Mrs. Dash, Onion + Herb (1 teaspoon)
- Frozen Petite Peas (1 lb)
- Garbanzo Beans (1 can, 15.5 oz, S.W. brand, low sodium)
- Peanut Butter (optional)
- Rice or Quinoa (for serving - multiple meals)
- Soy Sauce (3-4 tbsp)
- Brown Sugar (2 tbsp) or Honey
- Rice Vinegar (1 tbsp)
- Cornstarch (1 tbsp)
- Garlic Powder
- Italian Seasoning
- Salt and Pepper
